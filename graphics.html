Concept Drawings: Mandala Railway Station

Concept drawings for EkiBeki's @ekibeki_india Mandla Railway Station beautification project. Inspired by the stories of women freedom fighters Avanti Bai and Rani Durgavati, the designs explored themes of courage, resilience, and leadership.

As the murals were to be painted by Gond artisans, the artwork drew from the visual language of Gond art, incorporating its distinctive motifs, patterns, and storytelling traditions. The project aimed to honour these historical figures while celebrating the region's artistic heritage.
